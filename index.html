About Meal4All

Meal4All is a community-driven platform that bridges the gap between food donors and NGOs. Our mission is to reduce food waste and fight hunger by enabling restaurants, caterers, and event organizers to donate excess food to verified NGOs that serve people in need.

Every day, tons of edible food are thrown away. At the same time, millions go hungry. Meal4All is committed to changing this reality - one meal at a time.
